02 Overview

Making professional training actually scale

Skillz-up is an AI-powered LMS designed to help Moroccan businesses digitize their training programs. It turns existing PDFs, presentations, and procedures into interactive courses, quizzes, and audio modules in minutes — distributed via QR code, no app required.

The product needed a landing page that sells the AI value prop without alienating non-tech founders, a product UI that's simple enough for first-time LMS admins, and a multilingual experience (FR / EN / AR) that respects the local market without compromising on polish.

03 Diagnosis

Why training programs fail, and how we fixed it

✕ Challenges

  • SMEs still train "on the job" — no structure, no measurement, no compliance trail
  • Existing LMSs are built for big enterprises — too complex, too expensive
  • Course creation takes weeks of authoring work most teams can't afford
  • Distribution requires apps, accounts, IT setup — friction kills adoption
  • Multilingual market (FR/AR/EN) — most platforms only do one well

✓ Design Direction

  • "Upload → AI generates" as the core narrative — make speed the hero
  • QR code distribution to remove every barrier between manager and learner
  • Tiered pricing that grows with the team, not enterprise lock-in
  • Right-to-left ready type system and component library from day one
  • Dark, premium SaaS aesthetic with the purple accent owned consistently
04 AI Studio

Where one document becomes a full course

The AI Studio is the heart of the product — twelve content types generated from a single source file: presentations, quizzes, flashcards, mind maps, AI audio, AI video, glossaries, infographics. The UI surfaces this breadth without overwhelming first-time users.

05 Process

Research first, pixels second

01

Discovery

User interviews with 10 professionals across SMEs. Competitive audit of 6 LMS platforms. Defined personas and key jobs-to-be-done for trainers and learners.

02

Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes for core flows: AI generation, course discovery, lesson player, admin dashboard. Validated with stakeholders before adding visual layer.

03

UI Design

High-fidelity UI in Figma with full design system, tokens, FR/AR/EN variants, and responsive specs. Purple-forward SaaS aesthetic with strong AI cues.

04

Test & Handoff

Usability testing with 6 users. Iterated on AI generation flow and lesson player. Delivered annotated specs and Figma dev handoff for the Next.js team.
